## Chord building ##

* major triad - 1,3 ,5  - 1+4+3 - **happy**
* minor triad - 1,3b,5 - 1+3+4  - **sad**
* augmented triad - 1,3,6 - 1+4+4 (two major triads) - extra semitone at the 5th - **mysterious**, enchanted forest


## Inversions ##

2nd inversion - 3,5,1
